2. How will this vote change anything?

We want to reformulate climate change as a global topic that should not be left to politics.

Step 1 is for us all, citizens, to realize that we have the right to vote on how to deal with climate change simply because we live in this planet.

Step 2 is to finance the top solutions that people voted for so that our strategy translates into action.

In the end, it's us, the people, that should determine what to do with our planet, not others.

What is Blockchain and why are you using it?

To make the story short, public Blockchains are giant databases that don't rely on any single institution and provide tools to register information forever without the possibility to change it. We leverage this technology to show voters that their vote was casted as intended and to achieve a high level of transparency in the whole voting process. When there is nothing to hide, Blockchain works best :)

Why did we never vote
on how to deal with
climate change?

Everybody seems to have
an opinion on it
but we don't have a
collective strategy.

We are building a simple tool
for people around the world
to vote on global solutions

For users who verify their identity,
we will send cryptocurrency to both you
and the projects fighting climate change
that you voted for.

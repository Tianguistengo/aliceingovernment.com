BLOCKCHAIN vs CLIMATE CHANGE

Why did we never vote on how to deal with climate change?

Everybody seems to have an opinion but we don't know what's the collective strategy.

We are building a simple tool for every person on the planet to register their individual vote on blockchain

After you vote, we will send cryptocurrency to the project fighting climate change that you choose.
